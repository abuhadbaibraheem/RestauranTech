1. Introduction

RestauranTech, LLC. ("we," "our," "us") provides AI-powered phone ordering services for restaurants, including secure payment processing. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect personal information.

2. Information We Collect

From Restaurant Partners:

Contact names, emails, company details.

From Callers:

Order details, delivery/pickup preferences, and payment method (processed via PCI-compliant gateways).

Technical Data:

Call logs (with sensitive data redacted), IP addresses, device/browser info.
